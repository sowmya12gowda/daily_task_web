About Us

Sampoorna Institute of Technology and Research, Bangalore, Karnataka, popularly known as SITAR is working for producing young technocrats and fresh minds for research works. The institute is providing under graduate degrees in engineering course and postgraduate degree in Master of Business Administration.

Sampoorna Institute of Technology and Research, Bangalore Karnataka is established in the year 2010 as Private Educational Institute to provide higher education to build career in the field of Engineering and Management.

Sampoorna Institute of Technology and Research is having Affiliation with reputed AICTE
